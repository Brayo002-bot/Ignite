About Ignite SACCO

Ignite SACCO is a community-driven savings group where each member contributes a minimum of KES 500 per month. Contributions can exceed this amount, but you must meet the minimum to proceed monthly. Loans of up to KES 500 are available to members who have saved at least KES 1,000 and have cleared their last month's savings. Loans incur a 4% weekly interest. Only M-Pesa is supported for payments. Members are added by the admin, and no self-registration is allowed. No refunds are processed before the admin-set date. Reminders will be sent via WhatsApp for due dates.
